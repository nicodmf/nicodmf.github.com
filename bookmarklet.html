Bookmarklets

Bookmarklets are little javascript tools to help with repetitive browser tasks. To use a bookmarklet from this site:
  1. Drag the bookmarklet (e.g. bnc# search) to your Bookmarks Toolbar
  2. While viewing a page you want to use the bookmarklet on, click the bookmarklet from your Bookmarks Toolbar.
